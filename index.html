      "Back tp the Future"

      Back to the Future is a masterpiece of adventure, comedy, and science fiction. While it may not hit the deep, tragic notes of an epic finale like Endgame, its impact is rooted in pure exhilaration, nostalgia, and a universally relatable anxiety about identity and time. Ultimately, the film leaves the audience with a persistent feeling of wonder and hope.       "The Intouchables"

      Intouchables is a powerful and deeply moving French film that evokes a complex and rich set of emotions, largely centered on its themes of unlikely friendship, class differences, and the triumph of the human spirit over physical adversity. The film's strongest emotional core is the unexpected, beautiful friendship that develops between Philippe, a wealthy, aristocratic quadriplegic, and Driss, a street-smart young man from the housing projects hired as his live-in caretaker. While the film is optimistic, it doesn't shy away from the challenges faced by both men, which elicits strong empathetic emotions. In essence, The Intouchables takes you on a journey from pity and loneliness to unbridled joy and inspiration, making it one of the most emotionally impactful films about the human spirit.
